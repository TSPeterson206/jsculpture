New Life

How can we not be awed by the majesty of whales? The wonder of seeing whales has spawned an entire industry to view them. This sculpture is an example of the stone determining the outcome. As I carved this stone, the forms appeared. I had no idea what the end result would be when I began, but the result complemented the coloring of the stone as if it was meant to be.
