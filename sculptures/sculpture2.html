Time Out

Limestone is an organic sedimentary rock made primarily of calcium carbonate from ocean water. It has been used in carving and architecture for thousands of years. This hand-carved piece is representative of an animal that has recently been in the news as we invade its habitat and are forced to interact with this majestic animal. The cat in this sculpture is resting as it contemplates its next hunt, or possibly how to avoid any interaction with those invading its territory.
